Youtube

Authored By: Urvish Trivedi

Started by Jawed Karim, Steve Chen, and Chad Hurley, YouTube first launched in 2005 and has now become one of the most visited websites in the history of the internet. As employees of PayPal, the three soon realized, in 2004, that there wasn't one location where videos could be shared.

YouTube was originally created as a platform for anyone to post any video content they desired. It was hoped that users could use the site to upload, share, and view content without restriction. It has since grown to become one of the foremost video distribution sites in the world.

In 2006, YouTube was purchased by Google, "for $1.65 billion in a stock-for-stock transaction". 3 YouTube still operates independently from Google to keep the community that has been with YouTube from the start. Steve and Chad are now the CEO and Chief Technology Officer of YouTube, respectively.
